1. How has the scientific method helped us in our fight against diseases?

The scientific method has helped us to solve such problems as the causes of diseases and their prevention. Today, babies are born in hospitals where they are protected against common childhood diseases. Now, a person can expect to live to be almost seventy years old.

2. Write a note on better sanitary conditions available in our cities today.

Today our city streets are paved and well-drained. They are cleaned regularly. Sewage is carried through sealed pipes to disposal plants.

3. What are the sanitary conditions like in our village today and how would you improve them?

Sanitary conditions in our villages are very bad. The streets are unpaved and poorly drained. For improvement, the sanitation departments should be activated in the villages. The villagers should be enlightened about the dangers of diseases caused by the unsanitary conditions.

4. How has the scientific method helped us in the production and preservation of food?

The scientific method has helped us in producing a variety of food. Not only is the food produced in abundance, but also is preserved in a way way that it is both nutritious and enjoyable. Regardless of where people live today, they can obtain fresh fruits, meat and vegetables throughout the year.

5. We are now generally less fearful than our ancestors were. What were our ancestors afraid of?

Our ancestors were superstitious and they were afraid of the signs of bad luck. These superstitions were black cats, broken mirrors and the number 13.

6. How has the scientific method enabled us to get over the old fears?

The use of scientific method has enabled us to get over the old fear by cultivating in us the attitude of open-mindedness. It has demonstrated that there is always a good natural reason for everything that happens to people. As a result, most people no longer fear black cats, broken mirrors and the number 13.

7. What part did astrology play in the lives of men and women in the past? Give examples.

Astrology greatly influenced the lives of the people in the past. Most people were superstitious and linked matters like birth, death, disease and marriage to the supernatural world. They followed the movements of the stars to settle the important matters of their life.

8. Describe some of the superstitions still current in our country. How do they affect the lives of those who believe in them?

Many people, specially the uneducated and the villagers, still believe in illogical things. They still believe in the magical powers of the charms. They consider black cats, broken mirrors/images and the number 13 ominous. Their lives are greatly affected by these superstitions. They set their tasks on the basis of their false beliefs.
